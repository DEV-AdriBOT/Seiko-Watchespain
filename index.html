Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all unworn watches in their original condition with all packaging and tags intact. Customized watches are final sale unless there is a manufacturing defect.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping within Spain takes 2-3 business days. International shipping typically takes 5-7 business days. For custom watches, please allow 3-5 business days for production before shipping.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination. All international shipments may be subject to customs fees and import taxes which are the responsibility of the customer.

How does the customization process work?

You can customize any watch by clicking the "Personalize" button on the product. This will open our customization tool where you can select different components, colors, and materials. Once you're satisfied with your design, you can add it to your cart or contact us for further assistance.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. For large orders or custom pieces, we may offer additional payment options.
